Ownership Model

Learn about our unique ownership structure and investment opportunities

The NSBLPA offers a unique ownership model that allows investors to participate in the growth of sales-focused sports entertainment. Our structure is designed to provide value to team owners, players, and the broader sales community.

Revenue Sharing

Team owners participate in league-wide revenue sharing from broadcasting, merchandise, and sponsorships.

Community Building

Ownership includes access to exclusive networking events with sales leaders and professionals.

Player Development

Invest in the growth of sales professionals who are also talented basketball players.

Investment Opportunities

We offer multiple tiers of ownership participation, from individual team ownership to league-wide investment opportunities. Our model is designed to provide both financial returns and professional networking benefits.

Team Ownership

Become the principal owner of one of our 7 professional teams, with full control over branding, roster decisions, and local revenue streams.

League Partnership

Invest in the league itself and share in the success of all teams through centralized revenue sharing and sponsorship deals.

Supporting Investor

Participate with a smaller investment that still provides access to networking events and a share of league profits.
